Scrambled Eggs

How to Make Scrambled Eggs

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience...and the proper pan. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to show you how to make scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 eggs
  • 4 to 6 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley and other herbs to your liking

Steps

  1. Crack eggs on a flat surface (helps ensure shells stay out of the mix) into a bowl that's deep enough for some serious whisking.
  2. Add liquid(optional)
  3. Heat the skillet
  4. Add the eggs and stir
  5. Add herbs and spices
  6. Gently scrap the bottom of the pan, turning the eggs until almost all of the egg mixture has cooked through but there are still a few wet areas.
